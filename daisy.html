Meet Daisy

A sweet, intelligent 3-year-old female Dalmatian longing for her forever family

Daisy’s spots aren’t the only thing that make her unique — with her graceful stride and gentle eyes, she’s captured everyone’s heart. She loves exploring open fields, learning new tricks, and curling up beside you at day’s end. Already housetrained and quick to learn, Daisy is eager to become your loyal companion. With her elegant spots and loving nature, she’s the perfect addition to any home. Could you be Daisy’s perfect match?

Daisy's Story

From Rescue to Radiance

Daisy was found tied to a fence at a park, frightened and alone. A Good Samaritan noticed her hopeful eyes and brought her to our shelter, where we discovered she’d never been spayed or vaccinated.

In three months under our care, Daisy has blossomed. She’s gained weight, mastered sit, stay, and paw, and now greets every volunteer with a joyful wag and playful bounce.

Daisy is now microchipped, and fully vaccinated. Her bright spirit and gentle nature make her a standout, and she’s ready to bring her unique energy and love into a forever home.

Microchipped Vaccinated

Perfect Companion

Daisy is the epitome of Dalmatian charm — energetic, affectionate, and fiercely loyal. She enjoys:

  • Going on scenic car rides with the windows down
  • Tug-of-war with her favorite rope toy
  • Enjoying sunbaths on the porch
  • Curling up in a cozy blanket for naps

Daisy would do best in a home without small pets or livestock—her curiosity can get the best of her! She’s excellent with other dogs after proper introductions and adores children with whom she can play her heart out.
