Meet Lily

A gentle, loving 4-year-old Black Labrador looking for her forever home

Lily is a pure ray of sunshine! With her soulful eyes and gentle demeanor, she's won the hearts of everyone at the shelter. Lily loves nothing more than being by your side, whether it's for playtime or cuddle time.

Lily's Story

From Rescue to Recovery

Lily was found wandering as a stray, underweight and frightened. A good Samaritan brought her to our shelter where we discovered she had a microchip. Sadly, her previous family couldn't be located.

During her 3 months with us, Lily has transformed! She's gained a healthy weight, learned basic commands, and most importantly, she's learned to trust people again. Her tail never stops wagging now!

Lily is current on all vaccinations, spayed, and microchipped. She's ready to start her new life with a loving family.

Fully Vaccinated Spayed Microchipped

Perfect Companion

Lily is the quintessential Labrador - friendly, gentle, and eager to please. She enjoys:

  • Long walks and exploring nature trails
  • Playing fetch - she'll retrieve until your arm gets tired!
  • Splashing in water (she adores swimming)
  • Cuddling up on the couch for movie nights

Lily would do best in a home without small cats or other small pets. She gets along well with most dogs but will need slow introductions.
