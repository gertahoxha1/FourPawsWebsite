Our Story

Welcome to Four Paws, where every wagging tail has a story to tell and every pawprint leaves a mark on our hearts. At our dog adoption center, we're not just finding homes for dogs, we're creating families and fostering a community of passionate animal lovers.

Founded in 2010, we've grown from a small local shelter to one of the most trusted adoption centers in the region, having successfully placed over 5,000 dogs in loving forever homes.

Our Mission

Four Paws was born out of a simple yet powerful mission - to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome dogs in need while advocating for their well-being. We believe that every dog deserves a loving and forever home, and we're dedicated to making that happen, one tail at a time.

Our Vision

Our vision is a world where no dog is left behind, where their unconditional love and companionship are cherished by families who understand the profound joy of having a furry friend by their side. We strive to create a society where animal welfare is a shared responsibility.
