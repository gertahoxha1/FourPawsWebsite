Meet Noodle

A sweet, gentle 9-year-old female Goldendoodle looking for her forever family

Noodle's warm soul and affectionate nature shine at every moment. She loves gentle strolls, soft squeaky toys, and cozy cuddle sessions. Well-behaved and loving, Noodle will thrive in a calm, caring home where she can share her heart with you.

Noodle's Story

A Heart Full of Gold

Noodle enjoyed her early years in a loving home, but due to family changes, she joined our shelter at age 8—still hopeful and sweet.

Over the past year, Noodle has charmed everyone with her gentle nuzzles and playful spirit—she’s quick to learn new tricks and thrives on affectionate pats.

Now at nine, Noodle is healthy, vaccinated, and eager to share her boundless love with a new family.

Fully Vaccinated Microchipped Spayed

Perfect Companion

Noodle's loving nature and gentle demeanor make her an ideal companion. She adores:

  • Leisurely park strolls and sniffing flowers
  • Soft plush toys and gentle tug games
  • Cozy cuddle marathons on the couch
  • Calm playtime with children

Noodle prefers a home without small pets—her gentle curiosity is best focused on humans and larger furry friends.
