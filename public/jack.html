Meet Jack

A spirited, affectionate 10-year-old Jack Russell Terrier ready for a loving home

Jack may be a senior, but his energy and loyalty shine through every wag. He loves short walks, puzzle toys, and curling up beside you on the couch. Already housetrained and gentle, Jack would thrive in a calm, patient household where he can be your devoted companion.

Jack's Story

From Loyal Companion to New Beginnings

Jack spent his prime years by his owner’s side, providing endless joy and loyalty. When his owner could no longer care for him, Jack came to our shelter at age 9.

In just a few months, Jack has shown his loving nature—greeting volunteers with enthusiastic tail wags and mastering basic commands with ease.

Now at 10, Jack remains active and affectionate, ready to bring warmth and companionship to a patient, caring home.

Microchipped Vaccinated

Perfect Companion

Jack zest for life and adaptability make him a wonderful companion. He enjoys:

  • Short, brisk walks and sniffing adventures
  • Puzzle toys and treat games
  • Cuddling up for cozy naps
  • Gentle playtime with kids

Jack would do best in a home without small animals—his hunting instincts are strong! He thrives with consistent routines and gentle guidance.
